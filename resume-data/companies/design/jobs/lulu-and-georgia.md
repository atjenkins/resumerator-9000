# Design Concierge

## About the company
Lulu and Georgia is a home furnishings and design company focused on premium home products and design services.

## Role Overview
Lulu and Georgia is seeking a highly motivated, results-driven Design Concierge to convert high-intent inbound leads into qualified, booked design consultations and product-driven transactions. This is a quota-carrying, sales-first role responsible for driving measurable funnel outcomes—speed-to-lead, consult bookings, conversion, and exceptional client experience. The ideal candidate is a consultative closer who thrives in a fast-paced environment, communicates with polish and confidence, and can speak credibly about high-ticket home products and design decisions with style and authority. You'll be the first point of contact for clients exploring design support, setting the tone for a premium experience and building momentum toward purchase.

## Responsibilities

**Convert High-Intent Leads into Qualified Consult Bookings**
- Own and respond to inbound leads with speed, clarity, and a premium brand voice
- Qualify prospects thoroughly (budget, timeline, scope, readiness) and guide them into the right design service
- Convert interest into booked design consultations through confident selling and strong follow-up cadence
- Prepare clients for successful consultations by setting expectations and gathering key information upfront

**Drive Revenue Through Consultative Selling**
- Recommend tailored products and solutions aligned to client needs and Lulu and Georgia priorities
- Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities using shopper history and internal tools
- Keep momentum strong by proactively following up with warm leads and reactivating stalled opportunities
- Stay current on product launches, promotions, and seasonal assortment shifts to sell with precision

**Operate with Strong Pipeline Discipline & Accountability**
- Track lead status, outcomes, and conversion activity accurately in HubSpot
- Maintain clean pipeline hygiene and follow documented lead follow-up cadences
- Use performance dashboards and team benchmarks to pace toward quota and improve results
- Partner closely with Design Leads to support consult readiness and drive conversion

**Deliver a Premium, Brand-Forward Client Experience**
- Represent the Lulu and Georgia voice in every client interaction—elevated, warm, and design-savvy
- Balance urgency with empathy to ensure clients feel guided, not pressured
- Create visually compelling assets (Spoak, Canva, GSuite) that inspire confidence and support conversion

## Basic Qualifications
- 2–4 years of quota-carrying sales experience
- Proven success consistently meeting and exceeding targets in a fast-paced environment
- Strong consultative selling skills: qualification, objection handling, urgency, and close
- Design fluency and confidence selling within Lulu and Georgia's aesthetic and POV
- Proficiency in HubSpot, Zendesk, and visual tools like Spoak and/or Canva
- Excellent written and verbal communication with a polished, persuasive, client-first tone
- Experience selling luxury or high ticket products virtually, with the ability to build trust and drive conversion remotely
- Comfort working in a performance-based environment with clear metrics and coaching

## Preferred Qualifications
- E-commerce, design services, or home furnishings sales experience
- Background in design-related sales such as home improvement services, custom retail (kitchens/closets/cabinetry), or interior design support roles

## Success Metrics
- Consistent quota attainment through high-quality consult bookings and strong conversion
- High consult show rate driven by thoughtful qualification and follow-through
- Strong client satisfaction and repeat engagement
- Accurate CRM tracking and disciplined follow-up that supports forecasting and attribution
- Continuous improvement in funnel conversion metrics through coaching and iteration
